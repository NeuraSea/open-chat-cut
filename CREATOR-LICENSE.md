# NeuraSea OpenChatCut Creator License

The Creator License is a low-cost commercial license for independent creators
who need OpenChatCut for monetized content or paid client work but do not need a
team, hosted service, OEM, or white-label rights.

## Price

- CNY 18 per month or CNY 179 per year; or
- USD 2.50 per month or USD 25 per year.

Taxes and payment-processing charges, if applicable, are additional. The
billing currency is selected by NeuraSea when an order is accepted. Prices may
change for future renewal terms, but not during an already-paid term.

The planned Creator service also includes 100 monthly NeuraSea AI Credits when
the hosted credit preview launches. Credits are optional service units, not
software-license rights, and are governed separately by
[`AI-CREDITS.md`](AI-CREDITS.md). Local and bring-your-own-key workflows do not
consume NeuraSea AI Credits.

## Eligibility

The licensee must be either a natural person or a single-member business that:

- has one named OpenChatCut user;
- has no employee, contractor, or other person using the licensed software; and
- has less than USD 100,000, or the local-currency equivalent, in total gross
  revenue during the preceding 12 months.

Anyone outside these limits requires a Business, Enterprise, or OEM license.

## Creator production rights

After NeuraSea accepts payment and issues an order confirmation or license
certificate referencing these terms, the named user receives a non-exclusive,
non-transferable, non-sublicensable, worldwide right during the paid term to:

- install OpenChatCut on up to three devices controlled by the named user;
- use OpenChatCut for monetized content and paid client editing work;
- make private modifications solely for the named user's permitted work; and
- use, publish, monetize, and deliver exported media without royalties to
  NeuraSea.

Clients may receive exported media and project deliverables, but may not access
or operate the licensed OpenChatCut software under the Creator License.

## Not included

The Creator License does not permit:

- account or license sharing, team use, or use by employees or contractors;
- providing OpenChatCut or its functionality as SaaS, a hosted or managed
  service, API, editing platform, or rental service;
- OEM, white-label, embedded, or proprietary software distribution;
- redistributing OpenChatCut, modified OpenChatCut source, binaries, or plugins;
- sublicensing, selling, transferring, or assigning the Creator License; or
- removing or misrepresenting copyright, license, provenance, or trademark
  notices.

Those uses require separate written commercial terms from NeuraSea.

## Term and outputs

Creator production rights last for the monthly or annual term shown on the
accepted order. If the license is not renewed, new commercial production use
must stop when the term expires. Media exported during a valid term remains
usable and does not become subject to royalties or a later renewal requirement.

The Business Source License 1.1 continues to govern rights not expressly
granted here. An accepted order, invoice, or license certificate may add terms;
if it conflicts with this document, the signed or expressly accepted order
controls. This public document alone does not activate a Creator License.
